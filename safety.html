Safety Guide

Essential guidance for floods, earthquakes, cyclones, and fires.

Floods

  • Move to higher ground immediately when warnings are issued.
  • Never walk, swim, or drive through flood water.
  • Stay off bridges over fast-moving water.
  • Disconnect electrical appliances if safe to do so.
  • Store drinking water in clean containers.
  • Keep important documents in waterproof bags.

Earthquakes

  • Drop to the ground, take cover, and hold on.
  • Stay away from windows and exterior walls.
  • If outdoors, move to a clear area.
  • If driving, pull over and stop safely.
  • Check for injuries and damage after shaking stops.
  • Be prepared for aftershocks.

Cyclones

  • Secure outdoor objects and board up windows.
  • Stay indoors in an interior room away from windows.
  • Stock water, food, batteries, and first aid supplies.
  • Follow official evacuation orders.
  • Do not go outside during the eye of the storm.
  • Avoid damaged buildings and power lines afterward.

Fires

  • Evacuate immediately when ordered by authorities.
  • Close windows, vents, and doors to limit fire spread.
  • Crawl low under smoke to breathe cleaner air.
  • Never re-enter a burning building.
  • Keep a cloth over your nose and mouth if needed.
  • Practice an emergency evacuation plan in advance.
